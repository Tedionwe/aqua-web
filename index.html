About Us

The fascinating coast of Taormina dominating the “Pearl of the Ionic”, offers not only historic wealth but also an underwater seascape rich with fish, shellfish typical of the Mediterranean fauna. The team at Aqua Man Diving Center works hard to assure you the most interesting, fun, safe, and best diving in Taormina.
We are passionate about diving, and hope to share our love of the sea with you. We welcome snorkelers, divers of all levels, children of all ages, families and everyone who wants a chance to enjoy the best diving in Taormina.
